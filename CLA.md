# Contributor Licence Agreement (CLA)

> ⚠️ **Draft for legal review.** This CLA is a starting point modelled on common
> inbound-licence practice. Have a solicitor review it before accepting external
> contributions under it.

## Why this exists

The SHD Systems Installer Kit is **dual-licensed** (AGPLv3 + commercial). To keep
offering a commercial licence, **SHD Systems Ltd must hold the necessary rights to
every line of code in the project**. If contributions came in under AGPL only,
SHD could not include them in the commercially-licensed version. This CLA solves
that by granting SHD the rights it needs, while you keep ownership of your work.

## The agreement

By submitting a contribution (a pull request, patch, or any code, documentation,
or other material) to this project, **you agree to the following**:

1. **You own or have the right to submit it.** The contribution is your original
   work, or you have sufficient rights to submit it under these terms, and it does
   not knowingly infringe any third party's rights.

2. **Copyright licence.** You grant SHD Systems Ltd a perpetual, worldwide,
   non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright licence to reproduce,
   modify, prepare derivative works of, publicly display, sublicense, and
   distribute your contribution **and derivative works, under any licence,
   including both the AGPLv3 and SHD's commercial licence** (i.e. you permit SHD
   to relicense your contribution as part of the project).

3. **Patent licence.** You grant SHD Systems Ltd and recipients of the software a
   perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable patent licence
   to make, use, sell, and distribute your contribution, for any patent claims you
   can license that are necessarily infringed by your contribution.

4. **You retain ownership.** You keep all right, title, and interest in your
   contribution. This CLA is a licence, not an assignment.

5. **No obligation.** SHD Systems Ltd is not obliged to use or include your
   contribution.

6. **As-is.** Unless required by law, your contribution is provided without
   warranty of any kind.

## How to accept

Until an automated CLA process is in place, include the following line in your
pull-request description (with your real name and the date):

```
I have read and agree to the Contributor Licence Agreement (CLA.md).
Signed-off-by: <Your Name> <your@email>  (YYYY-MM-DD)
```
