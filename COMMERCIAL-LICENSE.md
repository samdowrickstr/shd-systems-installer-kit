## Summary

The SHD Systems Installer Kit is dual-licensed (see [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md)).
The default licence is the **GNU AGPLv3**. This document describes the alternative
**commercial licence**, offered by **SHD Systems Ltd**, for parties who do not
wish to comply with the AGPLv3.

## What the commercial licence is intended to grant

Subject to payment of the agreed fee, SHD Systems Ltd intends to grant the
licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the Software **without**
the following AGPLv3 obligations:

1. **No copyleft / source-disclosure obligation** — the licensee may build and
   distribute closed-source products incorporating the Software, and may run
   modified network-facing versions without publishing their source.
2. **White-label / attribution waiver** — the licensee may remove or replace the
   "Powered by SHD Systems" Attribution Notice required by
   [ATTRIBUTION.md](ATTRIBUTION.md), and may enable the `SHD_WHITELABEL` /
   `COMMERCIAL` build.
